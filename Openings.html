The Materials Modeling Lab (MML) at IIT BHU offers exciting research opportunities for PhD candidates. Join us to explore cutting-edge topics in computational materials science and metallurgy.

PhD Positions

MML in the Department of Metallurgical Engineering at IIT BHU invites applications for PhD positions. Our lab provides a dynamic research environment with opportunities in:

  • Computational Thermodynamics
  • Experimental and DFT-based thermochemical data
  • Cluster Variation Method and Cluster Expansion based Gibbs energy models
  • Development of CALPHAD databases for novel materials and processes
  • Machine learning in materials design
