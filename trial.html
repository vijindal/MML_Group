PhD Positions

The Materials Modeling Lab (MML) in the Department of Metallurgical Engineering at IIT BHU invites applications for PhD positions. MML offers a dynamic research environment with opportunities in:

Research Interests:

  • Computational Thermodynamics
  • Experimental and DFT-based thermochemical data
  • Cluster Variation Method and Cluster Expansion based Gibbs energy models
  • Development of CALPHAD databases for novel materials and processes
  • Machine learning in materials design

To Apply:

Submit your application through the Institute website: ADMISSIONS (iitbhu.ac.in). Also, send your CV to Dr. Vikas Jindal (vjindal.met@iitbhu.ac.in) by November 15, 2024.
